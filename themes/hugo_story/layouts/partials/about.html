About Brendan Cullen

I am a geospatial professional bridging the gap between geography, planning, and programming. I have always loved maps, technology, and the uniqueness of place. Since graduating with a B.A. in in Geography and Environmental Studies, I have discovered the fascinating world of data science and visualization. I am using this blog to share my journey towards becoming a geospatial data scientist. I hope you enjoy it, and if you have any questions, feel free to reach out!

I am currently a Planning Technician at the Chautauqua County Division of Planning & Community Development. Opinions are my own.
